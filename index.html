About RNS First Grade College

Experience Excellence in Education - One Institution, Many Opportunities for Growth

RNS First Grade College, established in 2012, is one of Karnataka’s premier institutions renowned for its unwavering dedication to delivering outstanding education. Founded by the distinguished industrialist and philanthropist Dr. R. N. Shetty, our college holds an ‘A’ Grade from NAAC, reflecting our commitment to academic excellence.

At RNSFGC, a culture of experiential learning is championed, setting exemplary standards in various disciplines. The institution is celebrated for its value-driven education, bolstered by a team of highly knowledgeable faculty, cutting-edge laboratories, and a dynamic range of sports activities.

Achievements

Our college is celebrated for academic excellence and innovation. We continue to set standards of success across multiple domains.

  • NAAC Accredited with 'A' Grade
  • Autonomous Status Achieved
  • Top National Rankings in IT and Management Programs
  • Excellence in Sports with Regular Tournaments and Events

Sports

Our students thrive in a range of sports activities, from track and field to team games. RNSFGC encourages students to maintain a healthy balance between academics and physical fitness.

Join us for annual sports days filled with competitions that bring out the champion spirit!

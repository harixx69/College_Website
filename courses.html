Our Courses

Bachelor of Computer Applications (BCA)

The BCA program provides a strong foundation in computer science, programming languages, data structures, and software development. It is ideal for students aiming for careers in IT, software, and web development.

Bachelor of Business Administration (BBA)

The BBA program covers business management, accounting, marketing, finance, and entrepreneurship. It prepares students for managerial roles or further studies in business and administration.

Bachelor of Science (BSc)

The BSc program is designed to provide students with a solid foundation in science, covering various subjects such as Physics, Chemistry, Mathematics, and Computer Science depending on specialization.

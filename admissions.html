Admission Information

Admission Process

Admissions at RNS First Grade College are conducted based on merit. Students must visit the college office for inquiry and complete the application in person. No downloadable forms are available.

Eligibility Criteria

  • BCA: 10+2 with Mathematics or Computer Science background.
  • BBA: 10+2 in any stream from a recognized board.
  • BSc: 10+2 in Science stream (PCM/PCB).

Documents Required

  • 10th and 12th Marksheet (original & photocopy)
  • Transfer Certificate (TC)
  • Passport-size photographs (4 copies)
  • Identity Proof (Aadhar Card or equivalent)
  • Caste or income certificate (if applicable)

Note:

No online forms are available. Students are advised to visit the college directly for the admission process.

Need Help?

For any queries related to admissions, please visit our Contact page or reach out to the college office.

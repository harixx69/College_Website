About RNS First Grade College

RNS First Grade College is an esteemed undergraduate institution offering high-quality education in BCA, BBA, and BSc programs. We are committed to academic excellence and holistic student development.

Our Mission

To provide value-based education that empowers students with the knowledge and skills to succeed in life and contribute meaningfully to society.

Our Vision

To become a leading institution in undergraduate education by fostering academic excellence, innovation, and integrity.

Why Choose RNS First Grade College?

  • Qualified and dedicated faculty
  • Strong academic foundation with modern teaching methods
  • Focus on student development and placement readiness
  • Encouragement for co-curricular and extracurricular activities
  • Safe and inclusive learning environment
